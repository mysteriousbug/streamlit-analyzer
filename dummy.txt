I am working on a new audit now - Privileged Access Management Audit
Scope
The audit will assess the design and operating effectiveness of key controls supporting the management of privileged identities (PIDs) through OneVault and identity-based secrets management in HashiCorp vault. Our initial risk assessment has identified the following audit scope:
•
Identification & Vaulting - Evaluate the completeness and accuracy of the identification and onboarding of PIDs across applications and infrastructure within the centralised privileged access management tools
•
Release - Assess controls governing PID release, including the appropriateness of validations prior to the release of PIDs, the appropriateness of users who can release PIDs, and password rotation
•
Reconciliation - Review the processes to identify application and infrastructure PIDs not vaulted within the privileged access management tools
•
Recertification - Review the processes to ensure the ongoing completeness, validity, and periodic recertification of PIDs
•
Risk Management - Review the identification, assessment, and monitoring of key risks and metrics related to privileged access management and processes
This is a Group-wide audit.
