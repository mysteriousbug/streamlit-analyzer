# TRAINING PROPOSAL
## Building AI Audit Capability

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY

As AI becomes embedded in our organization's systems and processes, our audit scope naturally expands to include AI system assessments. Today, our team would benefit from developing hands-on skills to confidently test AI systems for security vulnerabilities—prompt injection attacks, model extraction, supply chain risks, and agentic system controls.

This proposal addresses that capability gap.

---

## WHAT WE'RE PROPOSING

A 12-week, self-paced training program designed to equip all 16 ICS team members with practical AI security audit skills.

**Q2 2026: Entry-Level Training (Free)**
- OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications, MITRE ATLAS, NIST AI RMF, RAND, Google frameworks
- All 16 team members
- Target: 100% completion by end Q2

**Q3 2026: Hands-On Labs + CAISP Certification (USD 1,099/person)**
- Practical DevSecOps platform (self-paced, 60-day access)
- 15 labs covering prompt injection, model extraction, supply chain, agent systems
- CAISP (Certified AI Security Professional) credential for all 16
- Target: 100% certified by end Q3

**Q4/Q1 2027: Advanced Specialization (Optional, USD 900/person)**
- Modern Security AI Security Certification for AI Squad (3-5 members)
- Deeper expertise in threat modeling and agentic systems

---

## EXPECTED IMPACT

**By end of Q3, the team will:**
- ✓ Conduct AI system audits independently, without external support
- ✓ Execute real vulnerability testing based on hands-on lab techniques
- ✓ Apply OWASP and MITRE frameworks consistently across all AI audits
- ✓ Hold recognized professional credentials in AI security
- ✓ Establish a repeatable, scalable audit methodology

**Strategic benefit:**
- Builds lasting internal capability rather than relying on external consultants
- Creates competitive advantage in AI governance and audit expertise
- Enables efficient audits of future AI initiatives across the bank

---

## THE INVESTMENT

**Total: USD 17,584** (USD 1,099 per person × 16)

For context, a single external AI system audit typically costs USD 50-100K. This program establishes internal capability that pays for itself on the first engagement.

Optional advanced specialization (AI Squad): USD 2,700-4,500 additional (Q4/Q1)

---

## RECOMMENDATION

We recommend approval of this program. It presents a low-risk, vendor-managed approach to developing critical audit capability, with immediate team readiness by Q3 and strong strategic alignment with the organization's AI initiatives.
